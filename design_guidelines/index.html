The space

Welcome to Grace Ganga, a peaceful 2BHK homestay tucked away in a calm residential colony of Varanasi—far from the chaos and noise of the city, yet close enough to reach key attractions with ease. Both bedrooms are air-conditioned for your comfort, while the living room is airy and naturally ventilated, though it does not have AC.

The home includes a functional kitchen equipped for everyday cooking (Utensils). While there is no generator, an inverter ensures basic power backup during occasional outages—though power cuts are rare in this area.

One of the highlights of your stay is access to a shared terrace on the 5th floor (by stairs) where you can enjoy a beautiful view of the Ganga. Ideal for families, couples, or solo travelers, Grace Ganga offers a homely, quiet escape in one of the most spiritual cities in India.

NOTE: WE HAVE INVERTERS FOR POWER BACKUP. PARKING: WE DON'T HAVE PARKING AT PLACE BUT STREET PARKING IS AVAILABLE.

Guest access

You'll have full private access to the entire 2-bedroom apartment — no shared spaces inside. This includes:

  • Both airy bedrooms with cooling
  • A well-equipped kitchen with utensils
  • Living and dining area
  • Private bathrooms
  • Shared rooftop terrace (5th floor, stairs only)

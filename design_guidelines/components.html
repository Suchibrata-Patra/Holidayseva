About this space

Escape the chaos of the city and unwind at Grace Ganga View Retreat — a peaceful 2BHK stay just 2km from Assi Ghat. Tucked away in a quiet lane, this cozy home offers all modern comforts, a private balcony, AC bedrooms, and a fully equipped kitchen. Whether you're here to explore or rest, our calm and welcoming space is the perfect base to experience the real soul of Varanasi — peacefully.

The space

Welcome to Grace Ganga, a peaceful 2BHK homestay tucked away in a calm residential colony of Varanasi. Both bedrooms are air-conditioned, while the living room is airy and naturally ventilated. The home includes a functional kitchen equipped for everyday cooking with utensils provided.

Guest access

You'll have full private access to the entire 2-bedroom apartment — no shared spaces inside. Both airy bedrooms with cooling, a shared terrace on the 5th floor with a Ganga view.
